MSM'2025

1st International Workshop on Multi-Sensorial Media and Applications

Exploring Emerging Frontiers in Haptic Interfaces, Olfactory Displays, and Cross-Modal Interaction Systems

October 30, 2025 | Shanghai International Convention Center

Workshop Scope

This pioneering workshop addresses challenges in multi-sensorial systems encompassing:

  • Haptic signal processing & coding
  • Olfactory display system design
  • Cross-modal neural integration
Key Innovation

Bridging the gap between conventional audio-visual systems and emerging multi-sensory modalities

Technical Topics

System Design
  • Multi-sensory environment architecture
  • Tactile internet frameworks
Human Factors
  • Psychophysical modeling
  • Cross-modal QoE evaluation
