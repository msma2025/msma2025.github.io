Workshop Scope

The workshop focuses on multi-sensorial media and its applications. Compared to conventional multimedia, multi-sensorial media encompasses a broader range of signals obtained from sensors, including haptics, olfaction, human motion, and electroencephalograms, as well as other emerging media types. This workshop aims to attract contributions related to multi-sensorial media and its applications. Topics include but are not limited to: (1) Design/architecture/implementation of multi-sensorial media system/environment; (2) Human factors, ergonomics and psychophysical modeling in multi-sensorial media; (4) Coding and delivery of multi-sensorial media; (5) Analysis and interpretation of multi-sensorial media; (6) Multi-modal interaction and cross-modal analysis; (7) Applications of Generative AI in multi-sensorial media; (8) Network management, resource provisioning, and privacy issues; (9) Standardization of multi-sensorial media processing; (10) Other emerging applications of multi-sensorial media.
