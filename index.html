Workshop Scope

This pioneering workshop addresses emerging challenges in multi-sensorial media systems encompassing haptic interfaces, olfactory displays, human motion capture, EEG integration, and cross-modal interaction architectures. We welcome submissions across the full spectrum of multi-sensory system development - from psychophysical modeling to network resource optimization.
